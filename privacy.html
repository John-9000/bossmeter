Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Boss Meter is an entertainment application designed for fun and sharing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

Boss Meter does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

All scores and app usage data are generated and stored locally on the user's device. This data is not transmitted to any external servers by the app itself.

2. Advertising

Boss Meter may display advertisements through Google AdMob only when the user chooses to view commercials.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain data automatically to provide, personalize, and improve advertising services. This may include data such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Diagnostic and performance data

This data is collected and processed directly by Google in accordance with its own privacy policies.

Users may be presented with privacy and consent options such as Privacy Choices, where applicable.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Usage

Any data collected through third-party services is used for:

  • Delivering advertisements
  • Improving ad performance and relevance
  • Ensuring proper functionality of the app

Boss Meter itself does not use or store personal data externally.

4. Data Storage

Boss Meter does not store personal data on external servers. Any settings or generated scores remain locally on the user's device.

If the application is uninstalled, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

5. Third-Party Services

Boss Meter uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

Boss Meter is not directed toward children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

Since Boss Meter does not store personal data directly, such requests should be directed to the relevant third-party service providers such as Google.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of this document.
