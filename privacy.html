Boss Meter Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Boss Meter is an entertainment application that generates a score for fun and sharing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

Boss Meter does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The app generates a score locally on the user's device. This score is not stored on any external server.

2. Advertising

The app may display advertisements through Google AdMob when the user chooses to view commercials.

Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically in order to provide and improve advertising services. This may include:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Diagnostic and performance data

This information is collected and processed by Google according to Google's privacy policies.

For more information, please review Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Storage

Boss Meter does not store personal data on external servers. Any settings or generated scores remain locally on the user's device.

4. Third-Party Services

Boss Meter may use the following third-party services:

Google AdMob – used to display advertisements.

These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Boss Meter is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of this document.
