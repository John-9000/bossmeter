What is Boss Meter?

Boss Meter is a fun, lightweight app designed for entertainment purposes. It generates a randomized "boss level" score meant to be playful and motivational, not a scientific or psychological measurement.

No account is required, and no personal data is collected directly by the site.

All results generated by Boss Meter are for entertainment purposes only.
