What is Boss Meter?

Boss Meter is a fun, lightweight mobile app created purely for entertainment.

It generates a randomized “boss level” score designed to be playful and motivational, not a scientific, psychological, or professional assessment. Refined ad experience — no interruptions, even in the free version ads appear only when you choose, and in the premium version ads can be removed completely.

Free version includes:

  • Boss score rolls & tier system
  • 2 Boss themes (Classic, Gold)
  • History
  • Boss Commercials (non-intrusive)
  • Timer-end notifications

Premium version unlocks:

  • 6 additional Boss themes (White, Purple, Silver, Midnight, Ocean, Azure)
  • Instant roll & custom roll timer
  • Extended history
  • Ad-free option & premium Boss visuals

Boss Inspiration button

Provides a quote from a well-known successful figure once every two hours. Quotes are collected from public sources and are attributed to their respective authors. Some quotes may be paraphrased.

Boss Meter does not require an account and does not directly collect personal information. The app may display ads provided by third-party services (such as Google AdMob), which may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

All results are for entertainment purposes only and should not be interpreted as advice of any kind.
